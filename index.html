About

The Tiger Degen Gameverse is a P2E platform where you can play all your favorite Degen Games such as Dice Game, Roulette, Card Games and other innovative games developed by the Tigers.

Our Vision is to become the most trending and leading platform for Degen Games, High Risk – High Reward. Our vision can be achieved by developing quality games that can be enjoyed by the Degen’s of Near, while also focusing on creating a strong community to ensure growth.

The first Degen to be released is the Dice Game which will be released within 3 weeks after mint.

We will then work closely with the community to bring the best and most desired games to the Near Space. After the release of the first game Tigers Degen Gameverse will begin to onboard other DAOs into the Gameverse and allow their members to get same level of satisfaction as Tiger Members.
